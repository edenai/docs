When you call a model through Eden AI, your request is forwarded to the underlying provider, whose own policy governs what happens to it.

## How Eden AI handles your data

Eden AI stores no request or response content by default, and never trains on your data. You can choose to have us store your request **metadata** by enabling log retention in your [profile settings](https://app.edenai.run/settings/api-keys). See [Data Retention](/v3/data-governance/data-retention) for details.

## How each provider handles your data

The tables below summarise, for every provider on Eden AI — both LLMs and Expert Models — whether the provider may train on your data (**Trains?**), whether **Zero Data Retention** — nothing stored — is available (**ZDR**), and how long data is kept otherwise (**Retention**). When ZDR is in effect, retention is **None**. A **ZDR** of **Yes** includes providers that offer it on request. Providers are grouped by whether they can be reached on the [EU endpoint](/v3/data-governance/eu-endpoint).

<Note>
  This information was last updated on 29 June 2026.
</Note>

**Regions** — Amazon Bedrock routes to `eu-west-1` (Ireland); Google to the EU multi-region (Vertex); Microsoft Azure to an EU Data Zone; Mistral, OVHcloud and Scaleway are EU-hosted; Databricks, Nebius and Qwen are served from EU-region routing on this endpoint.

## EU data residency

To keep data in the EU, use the [EU endpoint](/v3/data-governance/eu-endpoint) (`api.eu.edenai.run`), which routes only through the EU-eligible providers in the "available on the EU endpoint" tables above. Pin your `model` and `fallbacks` to those providers, and confirm the live lists with `api.eu.edenai.run/v3/models` (LLMs) and `api.eu.edenai.run/v3/info` (Expert Models).

## Guardrails

You can create a guardrail restricted to providers that offer Zero Data Retention, so the content it screens is never stored. The list of ZDR providers is reviewed and updated every month as providers' policies change.

## Reduce your data exposure

- **EU endpoint**: restrict processing to the EU-eligible providers above.
- **Zero Data Retention** (nothing stored): Amazon Bedrock, Google (Vertex/EU route), Mistral, Nebius, Qwen, Cerebras, DeepInfra, Fireworks, Groq, Perplexity, Together. Pin your `model` and `fallbacks` to these.
- **Logs off by default**: Eden AI stores no content unless you enable log retention.
