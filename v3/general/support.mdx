Eden AI offers several support channels depending on the nature of your question and your plan.

## Intercom Chat

For quick questions, use the chat widget available in the [Eden AI dashboard](https://app.edenai.run/). On the **Self-serve AI API Gateway** plan, chat support responds within 48 hours on working days.

## Email

For detailed issues, bug reports, or account inquiries, email the support team:

**support@edenai.co**

Include your account email and any relevant request IDs or error messages to help the team investigate quickly.

## Status Page

Check the current status of Eden AI services and subscribe to incident notifications:

**[https://app-edenai.instatus.com/](https://app-edenai.instatus.com/)**

## Dedicated Support with SLA

Dedicated support is available on the **Advanced AI Platform** plan and includes:

- Dedicated support channel with SLA guarantees
- Help with debugging provider issues, routing decisions, and best practices
- Custom latency and cost optimization guidance

See [plans and pricing](https://www.edenai.co/pricing) for details.

## Professional Services

Need more than API support? Eden AI offers **Professional Services** with access to dedicated AI engineers who can help you:

- Design and implement AI solutions tailored to your use case
- Productionize AI workflows with monitoring and safety controls
- Choose the right models and providers for your requirements

<Info>
Professional Services are available on the Advanced AI Platform plan. [Contact the team](https://www.edenai.co/pricing) to learn more.
</Info>
